Admissions

Come to Learn, Depart to Serve

Entry Requirements

Admission at Eliana Trust School is based on assessment to ensure proper placement and academic success for every learner.

  • Parents may visit any of our four campuses for a physical assessment.
  • If you are far from Lusaka, learners may complete an online aptitude test and upload the results.
  • Our admissions team will provide full assistance once you visit the campus.

How to Apply

  1. Fill in the online application form using the provided link.
  2. The administration will review your application.
  3. You will receive feedback through your email.
  4. Alternatively, parents may visit their chosen campus and purchase a physical application form.

Why Choose Eliana Trust School

  • Highly qualified and experienced teachers.
  • We follow the Zambian curriculum and syllabus.
  • Cambridge courses are also offered.
  • Free extra lessons every Saturday and during holidays for examination classes.
  • Boarding and day school services with modern facilities, safe dormitories, and comfortable bed spaces.
  • Affordable and favorable fees, with discounts for parents who have more than three children enrolled.
  • A disciplined, caring, and student-focused learning environment.
