Entry Requirements

  • Admission at Eliana Trust School is based on assessment to ensure proper placement and academic success for every learner.
    • Physical assessment at any of our four campuses
  • Online aptitude test available for applicants far from Lusaka
  • Full guidance and placement assistance provided by our admissions team

How to Apply

  1. Complete the online admission form
  2. Our administration reviews your application
  3. Feedback is sent via email or phone
  4. Physical forms are available at your chosen campus
